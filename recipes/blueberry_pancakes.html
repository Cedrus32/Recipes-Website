Blueberry Pancakes

Light and fluffy blueberry pancakes.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • milk
  • eggs
  • blueberries

Directions

  1. Whisk together milk and eggs. Pour into flour. Stir until moistened.
  2. Spoon 1/4 cupfuls of batter onto hot skillet. Sprinkle with blueberries.
  3. Cook until bubbles appear on surface. Flip and cook until edge is golden.
