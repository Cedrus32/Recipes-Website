Lasagna

A delicious and simple lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • ground beef
  • spaghetti sauce
  • garlic
  • mozzarella cheese
  • cheddar cheese
  • ricotta cheese

Directions

  1. Boil water. Add pasta and cook.
  2. Preheat oven to 350F. Brown beef, season. Stir in spaghetti sauce, simmer.
  3. Combine cheeses, stir well.
  4. In a oven-safe pan, layer noodles, meet, cheese until filled.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
