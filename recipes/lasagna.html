Lasagna

Simple and delicious lasagna bake.

Ingredients
  • noodles
  • ground beef
  • spaghetti sauce
  • garlic
  • mozzarella cheese
  • cheddar cheese
  • ricotta cheese
Directions
  1. Boil water. Add pasta and cook.
  2. Preheat oven to 350F. Brown beef, season.
  3. Stir in spaghetti sauce, simmer.
  4. Combine cheeses, stir well.
  5. In a oven-safe pan, layer noodles, meet, cheese until filled.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
