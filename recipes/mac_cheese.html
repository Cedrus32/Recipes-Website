Mac & Cheese

Cheesy homestyle mac & cheese.

Ingredients
  • macaroni
  • butter
  • flour
  • salt
  • pepper
  • milk
  • cheddar cheese
Directions
  1. Boil water. Add macaroni and cook.
  2. Melt butter in saucepan. Stir in flour, salt, pepper until smooth.
  3. Slowly pour milk into butter-flour micture. Stir continuously.
  4. Add chedar cheese to milk mixture and stir until melted.
  5. Fold in macaroni.
